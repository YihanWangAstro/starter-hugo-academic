<h2 class="theme-head"><span class="theme-name">Dynamics &amp; Tidal Disruptions in AGN Disks and Star Clusters</span><span class="theme-count">10 papers</span></h2>
<p class="theme-intro">Dense stellar environments — the accretion disks of active galactic nuclei (AGN) and the cores of star clusters — are crowded enough that stars and black holes routinely capture, pair, and destroy one another. <h2 class="theme-head"><span class="theme-name">Massive Black Holes &amp; Gravitational-Wave Sources</span><span class="theme-count">8 papers</span></h2>
<p class="theme-intro">Massive black holes shape galactic nuclei, and dynamical interactions in dense systems are a key channel for assembling the compact-object binaries that gravitational-wave detectors observe.
